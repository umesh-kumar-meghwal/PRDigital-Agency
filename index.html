Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see measurable results from marketing?

For Paid Advertising (Google Ads, Facebook Ads) and Bulk SMS, results can appear almost immediately. For Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and long-term brand equity, it typically requires 3 to 6 months of targeted effort to build sustainable organic keyword authority.

How do you track the success of our active campaigns?

We deploy advanced tracking tools including Google Analytics 4, pixel integrations, and customized conversion tracking codes. This allows you to evaluate your total customer acquisitions, exact cost-per-lead, and absolute ROI.

Do we have to commit to long-term digital contracts?

We offer flexible monthly retainers as well as project-based milestones. While we recommend ongoing optimization to maintain digital market share, we prioritize client satisfaction over locked-in contracts.

Our Journey

How We Started & Where We Are Headed

PR Digital Agency was founded under the vision of our founder, Prem Nagar, who recognized a common challenge: many exceptional businesses struggle to construct clear lead pipelines or build authoritative digital footprints. Operating from Kota & Jaipur, Rajasthan, Prem established the agency on the principles of absolute transparency and data-backed performance, focusing entirely on measurable growth for local and regional businesses.

Under his dedicated leadership, PR Digital has grown into a trusted partner for regional healthcare centers, educational institutes, real estate developers, and local brands. By blending analytical marketing systems with creative visual strategies, Prem continues to guide our team in executing modern performance campaigns that help our clients stay ahead of the digital curve.
